FREQUENTLY ASKED QUSETION:

Are eyeonic glasses suitable for prescription lenses?

Yes, eyeonic glasses can accommodate prescription lenses. Simply bring them to your optician for fitting.

Do eyeonic glasses block all blue light?

Eyeonic glasses block a significant amount of blue light; however, it is important to note that they do not block 100%.

How do I clean my eyeonic glasses?

You can clean your eyeonic glasses with a lens cleaning solution or warm, soapy water. Use a microfiber cloth to gently wipe the lenses.

Can eyeonic glasses be worn by children?

Yes, eyeonic glasses are suitable for children. Please ensure you select the appropriate size for your child.
